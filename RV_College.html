About College - RV College of Engineering Bangalore (RVCE)

Rashtreeya Vidyalaya (RV) College of Engineering Bangalore is one of the leading technical institutes located in the state of Karnataka. It is one of the top private colleges located in Bangalore, Karnataka that is known for its high-quality education, premium infrastructure, and good placements. The RVCE campus is spread across 52 acres.

Students can check with the admission office if the university allows payment through both online and offline modes. The total tuition fee for RV College of Engineering courses typically ranges from INR 16,00,000 - 70,00,000, depending on the programme and level of study.

RV College of Engineering Bangalore (RVCE) - Highlights

  • RV College of Engineering is an autonomous institution affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum.
  • RV College of Engineering was established in 1963. Also known as RVCE Bangalore, it is the flagship institution of the Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust.
  • The MCA course is accredited by NBA, New Delhi while the other technical courses are approved by AICTE.
  • RVCE ranked 89 among Engineering Colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).
  • A total of 214 companies visited the campus and offered jobs to more than 910 students from various departments.
  • Research & Development (R & D) cell of RVCE encourages research in line with the Institutional Vision to improve faculty and students.

Entrance Exams Accepted by RVCE

RV College of Engineering takes admission through the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (K-CET) conducted by the Government of Karnataka through the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) or through the Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) undergraduate test. Management quota admissions are based on academic merit and interview conducted by RVCE.
