About College - BMS College of Engineering Bangalore (BMSCE)

BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE), established in 1946 by the late Sri. B.M. Sreenivasaiah, is one of the oldest and most prestigious private engineering institutions in India. With over seven decades of academic excellence, BMSCE has built a strong reputation for its quality education, research orientation, and consistent placement success. Located in the heart of Bengaluru at Bull Temple Road, Basavanagudi, BMSCE benefits from close proximity to India's top IT hubs.

BMSCE is an autonomous institution affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and is accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. The campus spans 15 acres in the historic Basavanagudi area and offers a vibrant academic environment with modern infrastructure and industry-connected programs.

Entrance Exams Accepted by BMSCE

BMS College of Engineering takes admission through the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (K-CET) conducted by the Government of Karnataka through the Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) or through the Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) undergraduate test. Management quota admissions are based on academic merit and interview conducted by BMSCE.
