About RV University Bangalore (RVU)

RV University (RVU) is a state private university established in 2020 by the Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST) - the same trust that runs the prestigious RV College of Engineering. Located in JP Nagar, Bangalore, RVU is a UGC-approved university offering innovative, interdisciplinary programs. RVU inherits the legacy of RSST's 80+ years of educational excellence and brings a modern, industry-aligned approach to higher education.

The university focuses on experiential learning, design thinking, and producing industry-ready graduates. RVU offers programs across engineering, management, commerce, design, and liberal arts with a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary learning and global exposure.

Entrance Exams Accepted by RVU

RV University accepts admissions through its own entrance exam (RVU Entrance Test) as well as through KCET and COMEDK scores for B.Tech programs. For MBA admissions, RVU accepts CAT, MAT, CMAT, and KMAT scores. JEE Main scores are also considered for engineering admissions. Additionally, RVU conducts its own aptitude tests for BBA, B.Com, B.Des, and BA (Liberal Arts) programs.

RVU Admission Process

As a university, RVU has its own admission process independent of VTU affiliations. Candidates can apply directly through the RVU website or through authorized consultancies like Vinayaka Consultancy.

The admission process typically involves: filling the online application form, appearing for the RVU Entrance Test (or submitting valid entrance exam scores), document verification, and fee payment. FAQs

1. What is RV University (RVU) Bangalore?
RV University (RVU) is a UGC-approved state private university established in 2020 by the Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST) - the same trust that runs RVCE. It is located in JP Nagar, Bangalore and offers innovative, interdisciplinary programs across engineering, management, design, and liberal arts.
2. What are the B.Tech fees at RV University?
The B.Tech fees at RV University range from INR 3,50,000 to INR 4,50,000 per year depending on the specialization chosen. The total 4-year cost would be approximately INR 14,00,000 to INR 18,00,000.
3. What is the placement record at RV University?
RV University has a placement rate of 85%+ with the highest package of INR 30+ LPA and an average package of INR 6-8 LPA. Over 100 companies visit the campus for recruitment including Amazon, Deloitte, EY, KPMG, Goldman Sachs, and leading IT companies.
4. Is RV University affiliated to VTU?
No, RV University is NOT affiliated to VTU. It is a UGC-approved state private university with university status, meaning it has the authority to design its own curriculum and award its own degrees independently.
5. What B.Tech specializations are offered at RVU?
RV University offers B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (120 seats), Electronics & Communication Engineering (60 seats), CSE - AI & ML (60 seats), CSE - Data Science (60 seats), and CSE - Cyber Security (60 seats).
6. What entrance exams are accepted by RV University?
For B.Tech admissions, RVU accepts its own RVU Entrance Test, JEE Main, KCET, and COMEDK scores. For MBA, CAT/MAT/CMAT/KMAT scores are accepted. BBA, B.Com, and other programs require the RVU aptitude test.
7. How is RV University related to RV College of Engineering (RVCE)?
Both RV University and RVCE are managed by the Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust (RSST), which has over 80 years of legacy in education. While RVCE is an autonomous college affiliated to VTU, RVU is an independent university established in 2020 with the authority to grant its own degrees.
8. Does RV University have its own entrance exam?
Yes, RV University conducts its own entrance exam called the RVU Entrance Test. About 40% of seats are filled through this exam. Students can also apply using KCET, COMEDK, or JEE Main scores for the remaining seats.
