About REVA University Bangalore

REVA University, established in 2012 under the REVA Group of Educational Institutions, is a premier private university located in Yelahanka, Bangalore. Spread across a sprawling 45+ acre campus, REVA University offers a vibrant academic environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure. The university is approved by UGC and accredited by NAAC with A+ grade. REVA offers a wide range of programs across engineering, management, commerce, arts, science, law, and architecture.

REVA University is committed to providing quality education through innovative teaching methodologies, industry-oriented curriculum, and a strong focus on research and development. The university has established international collaborations with universities worldwide, offering students global exposure and exchange programs.

Entrance Exams Accepted by REVA University

REVA University accepts admissions through multiple entrance exams including REVA Entrance Exam, KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test), COMEDK UGET, and JEE Main for B.Tech programs. For MBA, the university accepts CAT, MAT, CMAT, and KMAT scores. The university also conducts its own entrance examination for various programs.

Seat Distribution at REVA University

The seat distribution at REVA University follows a structured pattern: REVA Entrance Exam accounts for 40% of seats, KCET/COMEDK accounts for 35% of seats, and Management Quota covers the remaining 25% of seats. Students are advised to apply through multiple channels to maximize their chances of admission.
